# Privacy Policy — RecoveryAI (Missed-Lead Recovery)

**Last updated:** 2026-05-23
**Data Controller:** The contractor using the RecoveryAI service
**Data Processor:** RecoveryAI (your company name and registration details here)

---

## 1. What Data We Collect

When you call a business that uses RecoveryAI and they miss your call, we may collect the following data through an SMS conversation — **only if you give explicit consent**:

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Phone number** | Identifying you as the caller and communicating via SMS |
| **Name** | Addressing you properly in messages |
| **Issue description** | Helping the contractor understand your service need |
| **Urgency level** | Prioritizing your request appropriately |
| **Booking time** | Scheduling a callback at a time that works for you |
| **Satisfaction score** | Improving the contractor's service quality |
| **Conversation log** | Maintaining a record of all SMS exchanges |

We do **not** collect:
- Payment information
- Location data
- Email address (unless voluntarily provided)
- Any information from your phone beyond what you send us via SMS

---

## 2. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on **explicit consent** (GDPR Article 6(1)(a)). When you receive the initial SMS after a missed call, you must reply **YES** (or **KYLLÄ** in Finnish) to opt in. If you reply **STOP** (or **EI**), no data beyond your phone number is stored, and you will not be contacted again.

You can withdraw your consent at any time by:
- Replying **STOP** to any SMS from us
- Contacting the contractor directly
- Emailing us at [privacy@recoveryai.fi](mailto:privacy@recoveryai.fi)

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the purpose of connecting you with the contractor whose call you missed:

- **SMS communication**: Qualifying your service need and scheduling a callback
- **Booking**: Facilitating an appointment via Calendly
- **Service follow-up**: One satisfaction survey sent 24 hours after job completion
- **Contractor dashboard**: The contractor views your lead information to prepare for the callback

Your data is **never**:
- Sold to third parties
- Used for marketing purposes
- Shared with other contractors
- Used for profiling or automated decision-making

---

## 4. Data Retention

| Data type | Retention period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Active leads | Until the job is completed + 12 months |
| Completed leads | 12 months after last interaction |
| After retention period | Data is automatically anonymized (phone, name, and conversation content are removed; only aggregate statistics are retained) |

You can request immediate deletion at any time (see Section 6).

## 5. Data Storage & Security

| Component | Location | Provider |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| Database | Frankfurt, Germany (EU) | Supabase |
| Backend server | Frankfurt, Germany (EU) | Fly.io |
| SMS delivery | EU routing | Twilio |
| Push notifications | Global | Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) |
| Calendar booking | US/EU | Calendly |

All data is:
- Encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+)
- Encrypted at rest (AES-256)
- Protected by Row Level Security (each contractor can only access their own data)
- Accessed only by authenticated, authorized users

## 6. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject in the EU, you have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
- **Rectification**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of all your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restriction**: Request limitation of processing
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Object**: Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact:
- **Email**: [privacy@recoveryai.fi](mailto:privacy@recoveryai.fi)
- **Response time**: Within 30 days

If you believe your data protection rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman (Tietosuojavaltuutetun toimisto): [tietosuoja.fi](https://tietosuoja.fi)
